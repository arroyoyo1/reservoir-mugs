what makes me the person i am?

'there was a time when i didn't exist, but now i do'

loving

the best characteristic of human beings is the ability to give love without expecting anything in return. that's why i like to be thoughtful and remind my loved ones how important they are to me. in essence, loving is human

adventurous

i love meeting new people, visiting new places, listening to unusual music, it doesn't matter if it's hot or cold, if i'm at home or 100km away from it, any time of the year is good to go out and explore new things

supportive

i try to always be aware of those who need support, even if it is from something as simple as a chat or to pay a visit. i think it is important every day to propose yourself to be an agent of change, to make the world better.
